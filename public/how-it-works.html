How the podcast highlight editor works

The workflow is designed for long podcast episodes where the final editorial judgment belongs to a person. Automation prepares the options; the human chooses the cut.

Ingest the episode

A URL can point to an RSS feed, an episode page, or direct audio. RSS feeds resolve to the most recent enclosure, while other URLs are resolved through yt-dlp.

Transcribe and diarize

The resolved audio URL is submitted to AssemblyAI for transcription, diarization, and timestamped speaker-turn segments.

Find highlight candidates

Claude reads the diarized transcript, follows the episode’s conversation, and proposes high-signal highlights for review.

Review and select

Preview each suggested moment, select the highlights to include, and reject the rest.

Create the edited episode

The review page prepares each selected segment, applies short fades, and assembles the finished episode in your browser.

Built for human-guided podcast editing workflows.
