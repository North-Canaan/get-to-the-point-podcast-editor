Frequently asked questions

Does the editor work with Hebrew podcast episodes?

Yes. The transcription stage uses AssemblyAI with Hebrew language settings and speaker diarization, and the highlight-detection prompt requires Hebrew reasons for every candidate.

Who decides what makes the final edit?

A human editor does. The system proposes candidates, but the final approve, reject, reorder, and timing decisions happen in the review interface.

What kind of database is used?

Supabase is the recommended free-tier product for production metadata and audio artifacts because it provides Postgres and object storage in one managed service.

Are private job pages indexed?

No. Review pages, source audio, and output audio are excluded from the sitemap and served with noindex headers so public discovery focuses only on product pages.
